Token Meter

Live counter for ChatGPT, Claude & Gemini

Only needed for the optional "Rewrite with AI" button. Stored locally on this device — never sent anywhere except directly to Anthropic's API.

How counting works

  • ChatGPT — exact count (loaded only once you click into the chat box)
  • Claude — close estimate (Anthropic doesn't publish its tokenizer)
  • Gemini — close estimate (Google doesn't publish its tokenizer)
